## I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

1. Go to the login page: `{{app_url}}/login`
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the password field
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Check your inbox for a reset email — it arrives within 2 minutes
5. Click the link in the email (it expires after 60 minutes)
6. Set your new password and log in

**Didn't receive the reset email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Make sure you're using the correct email address
- Add `noreply@{{company_domain}}` to your safe senders list
- If still not received after 5 minutes, [contact support](#contact)

---

## My account is locked — what do I do?

Accounts are automatically locked after **5 failed login attempts** as a security measure.

**To unlock your account:**
- Wait 15 minutes — accounts auto-unlock after this period
- Or contact support to unlock immediately (see below)

If you're using SSO (Single Sign-On), your access is controlled by your company's IT administrator — contact them directly.

---

## I can't log in — it says my email isn't recognised

This usually means one of the following:

**Check 1 — Are you using the right email?**
Check if you may have signed up with a different email address (work vs personal, different domain).

**Check 2 — Was your account deactivated?**
If your company has an enterprise account, your admin may have deactivated your seat. Contact your internal admin or our support team.

**Check 3 — Are you on the right URL?**
Enterprise customers sometimes have custom login URLs: `{{yourcompany}}.{{app_domain}}/login`

## I'm getting a "Session expired" error

Sessions expire after **8 hours of inactivity** for security. Simply log in again.

If you're being logged out repeatedly within a short time:
- Clear your browser cookies and cache
- Try an incognito/private browsing window
- Try a different browser
- If the issue persists, [contact support](#contact)

## Two-factor authentication (2FA) issues

**Lost access to your authenticator app?**
1. Use a backup code (saved when you first enabled 2FA)
2. If you don't have backup codes, contact support with proof of identity

**2FA code says "invalid" or "expired"?**
Authenticator codes expire every 30 seconds — make sure your device's clock is synced. On most phones: Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically.
