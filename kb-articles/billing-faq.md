## Where can I find my invoices?

Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. All invoices are available as downloadable PDFs.

Invoices are also emailed automatically to your billing contact on the day of each charge.

---

## I was charged twice — what do I do?

We're sorry to hear that. This is rare but can happen if a payment method is updated mid-cycle. Contact support immediately at **billing@{{company_domain}}** with:

- Your account email
- The dates and amounts of both charges
- The last 4 digits of the card charged

We review all duplicate charge reports within 4 business hours and issue refunds for confirmed errors within 5–7 business days.

---

## How do I update my payment method?

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**
2. Click **Update Card**
3. Enter your new card details
4. Click **Save** — your next invoice will use the new card

Your old card will not be charged again after a successful update.

---

## Can I get a refund?

Our refund policy:

- **Annual plans:** Refundable within 30 days of purchase or renewal
- **Monthly plans:** No refunds for the current billing period, but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges
- **Feature add-ons:** Refundable within 14 days if unused

To request a refund, email billing@{{company_domain}} with your account email and reason for the request.

---

## How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel at any time from **Settings → Billing → Subscription → Cancel Plan**.

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. No data is deleted for 90 days after cancellation.

If you're cancelling due to a specific issue, we'd love to try and help first — chat with us before cancelling.

---

## I need an invoice with specific business details (VAT number, company name)

Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing Information** and update your business details before your next invoice is generated.

For previously-issued invoices with incorrect information, contact billing@{{company_domain}} and we'll issue a corrected version.

---

## When am I billed?

- **Monthly plans:** On the same date each month as your original signup
- **Annual plans:** On the anniversary of your subscription start date

You'll receive a reminder email 7 days before each renewal.
